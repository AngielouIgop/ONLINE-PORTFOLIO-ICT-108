B. A. S. U. R. A. REWARDS : BUILDING A SUSTAINABLE SYSTEM WITH A UNIFIED RECYCLING REWARDS APPROACH FOR AWARENESS

Rationale

The `user` table stores important information about each user such as their full name, username, password, contact details, role, and their total current points. Every time a user throws waste into the machine, the system records it in the `wasteEntry` table. This includes the type of material (like plastic, can, or glass), how many items were thrown, how many points were earned, and the exact date and time it happened. The type of material is defined in the `materialType` table. It lists the name of each recyclable material and how many points each one is worth. This helps the system calculate how much to add to the user's total points automatically. If a user wants to redeem a reward, the `reward` table lists all the available rewards, how many points are required for each, and how many are left in stock. Once a user redeems a reward, that transaction is saved in the `redemption` table, which shows what reward they claimed, how many points were used, and the specific `wasteEntry` it came from â€” including the date and time of the redemption. This structure ensures that every actionâ€”whether waste disposal or reward redemptionâ€”is fully tracked and recorded. It helps keep the system organized, accurate, and fair. With this, the barangay can promote responsible waste management while encouraging active community participation through a clear and rewarding process.
